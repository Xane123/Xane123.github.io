Tape Devices

In Windows 2000, you use Device Manager to control your tape devices. Device Manager provides you with information about how the hardware on your computer is installed or configured, and how the hardware interacts with your computer's programs. For information about using Device Manager, see Related Topics or click the Action menu in Computer Management, and then click Help.
