Art

While Xane somewhat wishes he could be an artist, his mind has annoyed him with fake itches that it can start creating within split-seconds for most of his life, keeping him from focusing or concentrating on more important tasks like drawing lines for art. Despite how this limitation is holding him back, he has signed up to a couple art-related websites to show the few pieces he somehow made when his mind wasn't bugging him too much.

Name Description
DeviantArt There isn't much art uploaded here, but Xane is on DeviantArt.
Art Fight Though he didn't compete in 2020's battle due to his mind stopping him (as usual), Xane's on Art Fight with one character, Susan, available to draw if you'd like!
Artbreeder When it was first released, Artbreeder showed what AI could generate (with its training data). Xane generated some interesting and sometimes outright gore-filled images using its first tool, Splicer, and has continued to try out and generate imagery using its more recent tools, his favorites being Splicer2 and Prompter. Xane doesn't like how AI is mostly ruining the internet, but he enjoys experimenting with AI images here.
Deep Dream Generator Xane can also be found on Deep Dream Generator. He used this website before Artbreeder, interested in the then-new trippy Deep Dream and somewhat artistic Deep Style "tools", running his own photos through them, admiring the mix of strange and interesting images that came from these experiments. Deep Dream Generator has added more models (including modern prompt-based generators), but Xane mostly sticks to the original duo. He has tried upscaling photos and generating a few Studio Ghibli-styled images of Mary and Mary's medium restaurant cup, though.
